Imagica generates images through a description with artificial intelligence with the possibility to choose different styles, compresses them and saves the selected images in your image library.

== Description ==

Introducing Imagica, the AI Image Generator plugin for WordPress! This powerful plugin harnesses the power of AI to generate stunning images based on your descriptions and chosen styles. Simply enter your desired description and select your preferred style, and the plugin will generate a custom image for you in just seconds. And best of all, the plugin will automatically compress the image and save it directly to your WordPress media library, making it easy to use on your website. With this innovative plugin, you can quickly and easily create beautiful custom images for your WordPress site with ease!

Major features in Imagica include:

*Generate images through an AI-powered description, allowing you to choose from various tested styles.
*The images are yours and you run no risk of copyright infringement.
*Select up to 4 generated images to upload to your WordPress library.
*The selected images are compressed for fast loading for your visitors.
*Images are automatically saved with a name and description in alt-text and description, following the best SEO practices for your site.

PS: You'll be prompted to get an Imagica API key to use it, once activated. Keys are free for try test; paid subscriptions are available for packeges images.

== Installation ==

Upload the Imagica plugin to your blog, activate it, and then enter your Imagica API key.

1, 2, 3: You're done!
